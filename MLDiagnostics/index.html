About Us

Our Goal is to offer highest quality services with most competitive prices.

ML Diagnostic Center is a privately owned medical facility that provides comprehensive and quality medical diagnostic services. It is owned and operated by a corporation composed of competent medical practitioners of different specialties, professionals and businessmen.

We pride ourselves in offering the widest array of services from routine laboratory tests, imaging, and multi-specialty doctors' clinics. We keep abreast of the latest technology to provide and perform the most advanced laboratory tests.
